Why Washington?

With the allure of big city lights and the serenity of pristine temperate forests, Washington offers the best of both human innovation and natural ecosystem. Explore our cities; hike through our forests; find your balance.

Washington Quick Facts:

  • Washington is home to 7,812,880 residents as of 2023.
  • Washington encompasses 66,544 square miles, and is the US's northeasternmost state.
  • Washintgon is ranked 7th in median household income.
  • Washington was the 42nd state to join the union, in 1889.
